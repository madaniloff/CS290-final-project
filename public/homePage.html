Omnaeus

Dreamt Into Existence.

When the great beholder Cephanox was cast out into the astral sea by a group of upstart adventurers, he was left with naught to do but sleep. In his endless slumber, he dreamt the world of Omnaeus into existence; it is a place of endless stories, manifested dreams, and inescapable nightmares.
